About Hayloga WedEx

We understand the importance of creating your dream wedding. That's why we've gathered an incredible array of vendors and suppliers under one roof, offering everything from exquisite bridal gowns and elegant suits to stunning wedding decor, sumptuous catering options, and much more. This is your chance to explore a wide range of wedding and household items, ensuring that every detail of your special day is covered. Not only will Hayloga WedEx be a haven for to-be-weds seeking inspiration and the perfect products and services, but it will also be a platform for exhibitors to make deals that surpass their annual sales goals. This event is an unparalleled opportunity for vendors to connect with a highly motivated and enthusiastic audience, resulting in mutually beneficial partnerships and success. See more...

But Hayloga WedEx is not just about shopping and business. We've crafted an immersive experience that promises entertainment and excitement for all attendees. Throughout the event, we will be hosting engaging activities, giveaways, and lotteries, offering you the chance to win fabulous prizes. The atmosphere will be electric, with a vibrant ambiance that sets the stage for a truly memorable celebration. Hayloga WedEx also marks a special occasion in the Ethiopian calendar as it takes place just a week before the Great Lent. Consider this event as a joyous welcome to the season, where you can connect with loved ones, revel in the festivities, and begin your wedding preparations with enthusiasm and joy. Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to make your wedding dreams come true. Mark your calendars for 1st to 3rd March, 2024, and join us at Friendship Park, Addis Ababa for Hayloga WedEx.
